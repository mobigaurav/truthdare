# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Thank you for using **Truth & Dare** (“we”, “our”, or “the App”).  
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the app.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide
- The app does **not require account creation**
- We do **not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or passwords**
- Favorites and preferences are stored **locally on your device**

---

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

We collect limited, non-personal information to improve the app:

#### a. Analytics
We use analytics tools (such as Firebase Analytics) to collect:
- App usage events (screen views, button taps)
- Feature usage (Party mode, Couples mode, Daily prompts)
- Anonymous device identifiers

This data is **aggregated and anonymized** and cannot be used to identify you personally.

#### b. Crash Reports
We collect crash and performance diagnostics (such as via Firebase Crashlytics), including:
- App crash logs
- Device type and OS version
- App version at the time of the crash

This data helps us improve app stability and performance.

---

### 3. Advertising

The free version of the app displays ads using third-party ad services (such as Google AdMob).

These services may collect:
- Device identifiers
- Approximate location (city-level)
- Ad interaction data

This information is used to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

Google Privacy Policy:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

### 4. In-App Purchases

Premium access is available as a **one-time purchase** processed securely through:
- Apple App Store (iOS)
- Google Play Store (Android)

We do **not** collect or store payment information.

---

### 5. Remote Content

The app downloads prompt content from a secure remote server to provide updated prompts.
- No personal data is sent with these requests
- Content is cached locally on your device

---

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Improve features and user experience
- Fix crashes and bugs
- Measure feature usage
- Display advertisements (free version only)

We do **not sell or share personal data** with third parties.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Truth & Dare is intended for users **13 years of age and older**.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.  
If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us.

---

## Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information.  
However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

---

## Your Choices

- Remove ads by purchasing Premium
- Clear all stored data by uninstalling the app
- Stop data collection by discontinuing app usage
